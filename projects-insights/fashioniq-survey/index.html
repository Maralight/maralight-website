About the Survey

Help shape a practical business management platform for fashion businesses.

This short survey is designed to better understand the operational, management and growth needs of fashion businesses and related operators in the sector.

Your responses will support Maralight’s research into FashionIQ, a business management platform being explored for tailors, fashion designers, bridal houses, ready-to-wear brands, uniform manufacturers and fashion schools.

Research Focus

Why this survey matters.

Fashion businesses often manage production, customer orders, staff, inventory, sales and growth planning at the same time. This survey helps Maralight understand those realities and design better support through FashionIQ.

The goal is to identify practical workflows, common challenges and business-management needs that can inform a more useful and grounded platform.
