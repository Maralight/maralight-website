About the Survey

Help shape a practical business management platform for fashion businesses.

This survey is designed to understand the operational, management and growth needs of fashion businesses and related fashion-sector operators.

The information gathered will support Maralight’s research into FashionIQ, a business management platform being explored for fashion businesses, tailors, fashion designers, bridal houses, ready-to-wear brands, uniform manufacturers and fashion schools.
